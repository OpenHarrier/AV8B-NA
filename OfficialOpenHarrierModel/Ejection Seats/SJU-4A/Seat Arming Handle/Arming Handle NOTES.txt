Parts needed:

(1) M5x200mm threaded rod
(4) M5 nuts
(4) M5 washers (optional)
(4) M5 split washers (optional)
(1) DAOKI Micro Switch AC 1A 125V 3Pin SPDT NO+NC Hinge (or similar)
(1) M5x40mm screw or bolt for pivot joint
(2) M2x12mm bolts for mounting switch
(2) M2 nuts for mounting switch

Assembly Order:
1)  Put M5 threaded rod through latching arm, secure in place with M5 nuts, optionally using M5 washers and M5 split washers to make it tight.
2)  Glue lower arm to upper arm.
3)  Glue arm assembly to the BaseWithFork (or BaseWithoutFork depending which one you want to use)
4)  Push the other end of the M5 threaded rod into the assembled arm.
5)  Put the spring on, then the 3D printed spacer, then screw on an M5 bolt, leaving 10mm or more of thread exposed at the end.
6)  Put the switch in, with the top edge of the spring contact facing away from the arming handle.  Secure with M2 bolts and M2 nuts.
